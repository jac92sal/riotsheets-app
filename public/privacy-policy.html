RIOT SHEETS - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

RIOT SHEETS ("we", "our", "the app") is a music transcription application that converts audio recordings into sheet music, chords, and tablature. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Audio Recordings: When you use the recording feature, audio is captured via your device's microphone. This audio is sent to our servers for music analysis and transcription only.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password.
  • Usage Data: We track the number of transcriptions performed for usage limits and service improvement.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and transcribe your audio recordings into sheet music
  • To identify songs, chords, and musical keys from recordings
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To enforce usage limits (free tier: 3 transcriptions per day)

4. Audio Data Handling

Audio recordings are uploaded to our secure servers for processing. Processed audio files are stored temporarily in encrypted cloud storage (Cloudflare R2) and may be deleted after processing is complete. We do not sell, share, or use your audio recordings for any purpose other than providing the transcription service.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

  • Klangio: For AI-powered music transcription and chord recognition
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For song identification and music analysis
  • Cloudflare: For hosting, data storage, and content delivery
  • Stripe: For payment processing (if applicable)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

6. Permissions

  • Microphone: Required to record audio for transcription. Audio is only recorded when you explicitly tap the record button.
  • Internet: Required to send recordings for processing and receive results.
  • Camera: Optional, for scanning sheet music (future feature).

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission, encrypted cloud storage, and hashed passwords. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. Audio recordings may be deleted after processing. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

9. Children's Privacy

RIOT SHEETS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
